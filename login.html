Planned Portal Access

A tailored experience for every NOVA community member

Each role will receive access only to the information and functions required for that user's responsibilities.

E

NOVA Explorers

Access learning missions, projects, feedback, achievements, schedules, and digital portfolio evidence.

M

Innovation Mentors

Review learner progress, support projects, provide feedback, organize sessions, and document outcomes.

S

Schools and Families

View appropriate progress information, participation, achievements, communication, and program updates.

Portal Priorities

Built carefully for trust, clarity, and growth

Portal development will follow a staged process before secure access is released.

1

Design

Define roles, workflows, permissions, and requirements.

2

Protect

Prepare authentication, privacy, and secure access.

3

Build

Develop learning, progress, and communication tools.

4

Test

Verify usability, permissions, accuracy, and reliability.

5

Launch

Release access gradually with training and support.
