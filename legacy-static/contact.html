School Pilot Conversation

A focused process from first meeting to potential launch

Schools interested in NOVA can begin with a practical, structured planning conversation.

1

Connect

Share your school’s goals, Explorer population, and current STEM priorities.

2

Explore

Review the three learning pathways and the monthly mentor-supported model.

3

Define

4

5

Launch

Begin with clear objectives, shared responsibilities, and measurable outcomes.

What Happens Next?

A simple and professional follow-up process

NOVA will use each initial conversation to determine whether the Academy’s current model aligns with the needs of the learner, family, school, or organization.

01

Initial Response

NOVA reviews the request and responds with relevant information or a request for additional details.

02

Discovery Meeting

When appropriate, NOVA schedules a conversation to understand goals, needs, constraints, and expected outcomes.

03

Recommended Next Step

NOVA identifies whether the next step should be family enrollment information, pilot planning, partnership development, or future follow-up.
