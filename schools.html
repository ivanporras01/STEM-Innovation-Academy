A Flexible STEM Partnership

Expand technical opportunity without rebuilding your entire academic program.

NOVA is designed to help schools offer meaningful STEM learning through a flexible combination of structured digital instruction, hands-on projects, and monthly mentor-led experiences.

The model can support electives, enrichment, after-school programs, career exploration, innovation programs, or customized school partnerships.

“
Schools provide the community. NOVA helps build the pathway to innovation.

Our goal is to complement the strengths of each partner school with practical technical learning and mentor support.

The Partnership Model

Designed for flexibility and measurable value

NOVA can be adapted to the schedule, student population, priorities, and technical goals of a partner school.

01

Structured Online Learning

Learners complete organized lessons, guided practice, knowledge checks, and project preparation.

02

Monthly Mentor Lab

One two-hour session each month supports practical activities, technical feedback, and project development.

03

Evidence of Achievement

Projects, skills, mentor feedback, presentations, and pathway milestones provide visible evidence of growth.

A Responsible First Step

Begin with a focused pilot

NOVA can begin with a defined group of students, one or more learning pathways, a clear schedule, and agreed success indicators.

A focused pilot allows the school and NOVA to evaluate participation, learner progress, project quality, mentor support, and future expansion.
